# AI Sales Copilot

An AI-powered Sales Copilot built using agentic workflow concepts in Relevance AI to automate company research, prospect intelligence, and strategic pre-call report generation for sales and solutions engineering teams.

---

# Overview

This project was created to help sales representatives and solutions engineers prepare more effectively for customer meetings by automatically generating contextual pre-call briefings.

The workflow combines:
- Company research
- Prospect/LinkedIn analysis
- Stakeholder intelligence
- Strategic sales preparation

The system generates concise and actionable reports that help sales teams understand:
- what the company does
- who the prospect is
- likely stakeholder influence
- possible business priorities
- recommended discussion angles

---

# Key Features

- Automated company research and summarization
- LinkedIn-based prospect analysis
- Stakeholder role identification
- Technical vs business persona assessment
- AI-generated pre-call briefing reports
- Multi-agent workflow orchestration
- Prompt-engineered sales intelligence generation

```text
                    ↓
          Company Research Agent
                    ↓
          Prospect Research Agent
                    ↓
         Pre-Call Report Generator
                    ↓
      AI-Powered Strategic Sales Briefing
```
